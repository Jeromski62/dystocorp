# Yugure

In the early days of humanity's expansion into the solar system, a small trading company called Yūgure Corp was founded in the colony of Eros, located in the Kuiper belt. At first, they mostly smuggled water ice reserves from one outpost to another, but the dangerous nature of the business soon made it necessary to hire mercenaries. These mercenaries brought opportunities for weapon deals, which allowed for the expansion of the arms trade and the subsequent loan of trained troops. Information trading and unscrupulous actions propelled Yūgure Corp to a position of power within just a few centuries.

---

But with power comes responsibility, and Yūgure Corp was not always responsible. Their questionable deals often involved the exploitation of entire star systems or even paramilitary action against rival companies. It was these actions that were responsible for the company's shady image. Yet, despite this image, Yūgure Corp continued to grow, expanding their reach to the point where they were more powerful than the governments of many solar systems.

If someone owned something of interest, Yūgure Corp's agents of the Resource Management Department (RMD) were dispatched to convince the owner that a sale or transfer of the resource (in whatever form) was in their interest. And if the owner resisted, the RMD had other means to persuade them.

In the end, Yūgure Corp became the most powerful intergalactic organization, with their reach stretching across the stars. Their name became synonymous with power, greed, and corruption. And even as the solar systems changed and new governments came into power, Yūgure Corp remained as powerful as ever, with their tendrils extending into every aspect of life, both legal and illegal.
