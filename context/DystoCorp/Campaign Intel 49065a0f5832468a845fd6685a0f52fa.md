### Intercepted Report of Yugure Corp

<aside>
🟥

Our latest mission on the moon "*DS-VY-ZB23 (DystoCorp Security Zone, Vega Cluster, Block ZB23)*," near Trade Outpost “*Vega Vista Plaza - Business Community Hub*" presented numerous growth opportunities and valuable insights into collaborative operations with unregistered subcontractors. The task was to meet with the subcontractor for the handover of an alien artefact of unknown origin and potential weaponry. The Contractors First Mate led the engagement operating independently from his Captain, whose lack of direct involvement highlighted some fascinating dynamics in subcontractor accountability.

During negotiations, minor misunderstandings arose regarding the pre-agreed pricing structure. This allowed us to gain practical experience in conflict resolution under evolving terms. Though the subcontractor opted to terminate the meeting and requested our departure toward the wilderness, this challenge became an opportunity to refine our resilience and strategic adaptability. Temporarily transferring ownership of our YGR Bulldog Mk2 Transport Vehicle to the subcontractor was a calculated step, and the Resource Management Department is already addressing resource recovery. The vehicle's value of 1,289 Yugure Credits has been documented for further processing.

While navigating towards the Trade Outpost, our team identified an entrance to a nearby tunnel system, presenting a valuable test of our agility and tactical skills. Unexpectedly, the subcontractor faced inquiries from local lifeforms regarding the unsanctioned removal of the alien artefact, which led to an unforeseen complexity. Our team observed how the subcontractor’s Captain struggled under the weight of dual negotiations. Ultimately, both he and the First Mate chose to leave the subcontractor, opting for a temporary retreat. We wish them success in their future endeavors.
Our office cat decided to stay on-site to play with a drone. Domesticated animals are a motivation booster for productive teams. Replacements are provided.

Upon thorough assessment, the alien artefact was deemed of limited strategic value and left on-site, while we secured a holographic projector and 200 Yugure Credits as mission assets
