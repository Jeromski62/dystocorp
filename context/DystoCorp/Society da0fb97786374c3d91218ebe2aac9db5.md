# Society

The impact on society is profound. People who are regularly exposed to these phenomena are struggling with the challenges of alienation and the loss of their social ties. Co-operation and empathy between individuals is put to the test as they have to learn to deal with the effects of time dilation and the resulting changes.

Companies respond to these problems by establishing divisions and offshoots in the various star systems to minimise travel between systems. Communication between the systems is limited to the essentials in order to maximise efficiency. Companies are developing special protocols and systems to optimise the transfer of data at the speed of light and ensure that important information can be transmitted quickly and effectively.

In this universe of corporate dominance and the challenges of space-time travel, people struggle daily with the impact on their lives and social ties. But against all odds, they try to navigate this new reality and find their place in a society shaped by the imperatives of spacetime and the machinations of powerful corporations.
